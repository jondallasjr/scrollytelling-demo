01 — Logo

The impossible connection.

Inspired by M.C. Escher's impossible buildings — two diagonal shapes that start on one plane and end in another, bridged by a horizontal element. Dynamic, yet balanced. Just like the connections we build.

Design story: The logo starts on an open grid — the same construction grid you see throughout this brand. Two diagonal shapes are drawn, inspired by Escher's impossible geometry. Those shapes are bridged by a horizontal element. The result is a geometric "S" that represents impossible connections made real. The symbol is then a geometrical representation of those connections — dynamic, yet balanced.

Usage Rules

✓

Use on high-contrast backgrounds

Logo must always read clearly. Place positive (navy) version on light backgrounds; negative (white) version on dark or colorful backgrounds.

✓

Maintain clear space

Keep a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "S" symbol on all sides. Never crowd the logo with text or other elements.

✗

Never stretch, skew, or recolor

Don't alter proportions, apply drop shadows, or use colors outside the brand palette. The logo comes in positive and negative — that's it.

04 — Design Elements

The building blocks.

Five recurring visual elements define the SimplaDocs brand environment. Used together, they create a system that's immediately recognizable — even without the logo.

The Construction Grid

The foundation of everything

The subtle cross-hatch grid is the same grid the logo was built on. It appears as a light background texture across all deliverables — the visual DNA of the brand.

The S Symbol

Decorative anchor

The logo symbol appears large and ghosted as a decorative background element — in dark sections, on CTAs, in presentations. It fills space with brand identity without overpowering content.

Diagonal Splits

Dark/light rhythm

Sections alternate between navy and light backgrounds, often meeting with a diagonal edge rather than a horizontal line. This creates energy and references the logo's impossible geometry.

05 — Voice & Tone

Confident.
Not corporate.

SimplaDocs sounds like a trusted advisor, not a vendor. The voice is direct, specific, and results-first — with enough warmth to remind you there are actual humans building this.

Brand Voice Traits

🎯
Results-first
Lead with outcomes. "11x faster workflows, 250+ hours saved" — not "we built an automation system." Real numbers, real impact.
📐
Direct & specific
No hedging, no fluff. Say the specific thing. "From 5-person startups to 500-person organizations" beats "companies of all sizes."
🤝
Warm expertise
"We spend the time to understand how you work" — not "we leverage our proprietary methodology." Expert but never arrogant.
🪟
Low-ego, transparent
"No black boxes, no technical dependencies, no over-engineered solutions." Let client quotes make the case for us.
✓ DO say
"Operations built to scale"
"Custom solutions" (not "software")
"Eliminate manual work"
"Replace scattered spreadsheets"
"Real-time visibility"
