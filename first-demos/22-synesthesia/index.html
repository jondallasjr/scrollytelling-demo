Project 22 — Synesthesia

When sound
becomes colour

Some minds experience the senses tangled together. Numbers have personalities. Music has shape. Touch has taste. What does this say about the nature of experience?

Experiment 03 — Paint Sound

Draw a waveform

Your strokes define the wave shape. The canvas is a synthesis engine. Draw freely — vertical position controls pitch, horizontal movement controls tempo.

Consciousness
is overlap

The synesthete's brain doesn't malfunction — it reveals something latent in all perception. Every sense is a construct. The redness of red, the loudness of loud — these are not properties of the world. They are experiences the brain assembles. The unity of consciousness is the ultimate overlap: all of this is happening in one place, at once. No seam. No gap. Just the experience.
