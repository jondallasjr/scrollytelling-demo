About this property

A neat three-bedroom townhouse within a secured complex on Masotchs Way, Waterfalls — offering privacy, convenience, and easy low-maintenance living.

The open-plan ground floor connects the fitted kitchen directly to the dining and lounge area, making the space feel larger than it is. The kitchen has direct access into the single lock-up garage. Upstairs, the master bedroom is ensuite; a second bathroom serves the remaining two bedrooms.

The complex includes overnight security and a dedicated 5,000-litre water storage tank — a practical necessity for Harare's intermittent municipal supply.
