# Area Research — Waterfalls, Harare

**Property address:** Masotchs Way, Waterfalls, Harare
**Research date:** 2026-03-13

---

## Suburb Overview

Waterfalls is a residential suburb located approximately **10 km south of Harare CBD**, in the southern part of the city.
- Source: [Zimbabwe Historical Facts on X (Feb 2026)](https://x.com/ZimbabweHistor2/status/2018894207654457729)
- Source: [property.co.zw Neighbourhood Alert (Jul 2024)](https://www.property.co.zw/blog/waterfalls-harare-neighbourhood-alert)

Described as "a vibrant suburb offering a mix of established homes and new developments in a convenient location."
- Source: [property.co.zw](https://www.property.co.zw/blog/waterfalls-harare-neighbourhood-alert)

The sub-neighbourhoods within Waterfalls include: Derbyshire, Grobbie Park, Houghton Park, Induna Park, Midlands, Mainway.
- Source: [City of Harare](https://www.hararecity.co.zw/places/tour/15/waterfalls)

**Character:** Green, peaceful, family-friendly. Noted for "tranquil atmosphere" and established community.
- Source: [Seeff Zimbabwe](https://www.seeff.co.zw/), [just.property.co.zw](https://just.property.co.zw/neighborhoods/waterfalls)
