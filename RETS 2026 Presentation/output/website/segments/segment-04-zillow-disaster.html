⚠
The AI only knew the good times
Zillow's algorithm was trained during a bull market. When conditions changed, it had no experience of "down" — so it kept buying at inflated prices.
◆
Automation without oversight is gambling
The AI was buying homes autonomously at scale. By the time humans reviewed the numbers, the damage was already $304M deep.
✓
The lesson for Zimbabwe's agents
Use AI as a tool, not a replacement for judgment. AI writes faster. AI responds faster. But you understand the local market — and that is irreplaceable.

Zillow had $500 million to lose. You don't. But you also have something Zillow didn't: local knowledge, human relationships, and the wisdom to know when the AI is wrong.
