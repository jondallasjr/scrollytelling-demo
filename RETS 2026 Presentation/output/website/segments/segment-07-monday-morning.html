Your Action Plan — This Week
1
Try AI for your next listing.
Free · 30 seconds · ChatGPT

ChatGPT is free. Paste your property details — bedrooms, bathrooms, location, special features — and ask it to write a compelling listing description. Compare it to what you'd normally write. You'll never go back.

Try this prompt: "Write a compelling property listing for a 3-bedroom house in Borrowdale, Harare. It has a garden, double garage, and open-plan kitchen. Buyer profile: young professional family."
2
Set up a WhatsApp auto-responder.
Basic setup · Protect your leads · This weekend

Slow response time is the #1 killer of hot leads. WhatsApp Business has a free built-in auto-reply. Set up a greeting message that responds instantly when a lead messages you after hours. Advanced: add an AI chatbot for $0–$30/month.

Start simple: "Hi! Thanks for reaching out. I'll reply within 2 hours. Which property are you interested in?" — that alone beats silence.
3
List everything digitally on Propertybook.
Foundation-building · Future AI training · Do this today

Every listing you put online builds Zimbabwe's property data foundation. You're not just selling a house today — you're training the AI that will dominate this market tomorrow. Agents who have the most digital history will have the most AI leverage.

Propertybook has 300,000+ monthly visitors and 2,000+ registered agents. Your listing is already reaching buyers. Your data is already working for your future.
